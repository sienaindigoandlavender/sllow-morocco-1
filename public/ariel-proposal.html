The harbour smells of fish and salt. The boats are blue. The sea walls catch the last light at six o'clock and the Atlantic goes pink before it goes dark. Your first evening is yours. Just the wind and the sound of the medina finding its night rhythm.

Essaouira

Day 2 — Monday, December 21, 2026

Riad Kafila
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Easy
Accommodation: Riad Kafila  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Activities: Medina tour, Horse riding on the beach at sunset

The medina is small enough to cross in fifteen minutes, which means you spend the day getting lost in it. Thuya wood workshops, their scent sweet and resinous, open onto the street. In the afternoon, gnawa music drifts from the square. Horses wait on the beach at four o'clock.

Marrakech

Day 3 — Tuesday, December 22, 2026

Essaouira → Marrakech
2.5h drive
Riad El Fenn
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Accommodation: Riad El Fenn  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch at La Pergola, Dinner at L'Mida
Activities: Sidi Kaouki, Royal Mansour Spa (late afternoon)

The road east moves through argan country. Silvery trees, dry hills, the smell of pressed oil through an open window. A long lunch in a garden before the city. Marrakech arrives with eucalyptus and diesel and then, suddenly, the pink walls.

Marrakech

Day 4 — Wednesday, December 23, 2026

Riad El Fenn
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Easy
Accommodation: Riad El Fenn  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner at Ksar Essaoussan or Dar Cherifa
Activities: Medina tour with official guide, Palais Bahia, Palais Badi, Medersa Ben Youssef, Souks, Jemaa el Fna at dusk
Guide: English-speaking official guide

A private guide who knows which door to knock on. The medina in the morning when the light is still cool and the souks are just waking up. By afternoon the streets are full. Spice stalls, leather, the smell of cedar shavings from the woodworkers quarter. Jemaa el Fna at dusk: smoke from the food stalls, a circle of people around a storyteller, the call to prayer cutting through everything.

Agafay Desert

Day 5 — Thursday, December 24, 2026 · Christmas Eve

Marrakech → Agafay Desert
1h drive
Scarabeo Camp
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Accommodation: Scarabeo Camp  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Christmas Eve Dinner at Scarabeo
Activities: Jardin Majorelle, Yves Saint Laurent Museum, Anima, Camel ride at sunset

Forty minutes from the medina and the silence is complete. The Agafay is not sand. It is pale stone, flat and vast, the Atlas filling the southern sky. In December the stars are excessive. In the morning, before the light changes, a balloon rises.

Tamnougalt

Day 6 — Friday, December 25, 2026 · Christmas Day

Agafay → Tamnougalt
5.5h drive
Kasbah des Caids
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Moderate
Accommodation: Kasbah des Caids  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch en route, Christmas Dinner at Kasbah des Caids
Activities: Hot Air Balloon at sunrise (Agafay), Ksar Ait Benhaddou

The pass reaches 2,260 metres. Snow on the peaks, nothing but sky above, the road cutting through rock the colour of rust. South of the pass the light changes. The valley appears below, narrow and green. A five-hundred-year-old kasbah, Christmas dinner inside its walls, the sound of drums carrying across the dark.

The Sahara

Day 7 — Saturday, December 26, 2026

Tamnougalt → Merzouga
5h drive
Dihya Desert Camp
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Moderate
Accommodation: Dihya Desert Camp  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch at Kasbah El Khorbat, Dinner at camp
Activities: Dadès Gorge, Todra Gorge, Lunch at Kasbah El Khorbat
Optional: short hike in Todra Gorge

Two gorges. The first cuts deep into the Atlas, the road narrowing until the cliffs are close enough to touch from the window. The second is narrower still. Walls rising straight up, a cold river at the bottom, the light arriving in a strip. Then the land flattens, empties, and the dunes appear without warning, orange against the sky.

The Sahara

Day 8 — Sunday, December 27, 2026

Dihya Desert Camp
Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Easy
Accommodation: Dihya Desert Camp  ·  1 Double, 2 Single
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch at foot of Erg Chebbi, Dinner at camp
Activities: Camel ride, Fossil hunting, Berber music evening (Ait Khabbach)

Nothing is required of you today. Walk into the dunes in the morning when the light is low and the sand still holds the cold of the night. The silence has texture here. In the evening, musicians from the community that has lived at this desert edge for generations play as the fire burns down.
